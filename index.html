Matilda

About Matilda

Matilda is a beautiful cat who originated from the streets of Catford.

Matilda is a boy cat.

Matilda has many interests, few friends and particular enemies.

Matilda's Food

Matilda's favourite food is:

  • Wet food

Matilda's eating schedule is:

  1. Whenever the human first stirs in the morning or can be seen through the window of the back door
  2. Around midday
  3. Whenever a human arrives home
  4. Whenever a human looks as if they are going to go to sleep
  5. As often as possible
